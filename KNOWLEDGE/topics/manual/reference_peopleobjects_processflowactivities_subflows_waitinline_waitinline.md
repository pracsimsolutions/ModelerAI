# Wait in Line

## Overview and Key Concepts

 

The Wait in Line activity will cause a person to WaitInLine to a waiting line and wait in it until they reach the front of the line.

 

 

The person will exit their current location, travel to the waiting line, and wait in line until they reach the front.

 

  

#### Sub Flow Activity

 

The WaitInLine activity is a Sub Flow Activity and its Inner Flow can be viewed if you wish to learn more about how it works, debug its behavior, or copy its logic.

  

 

 

## Connectors

 

The WaitInLine activity only allows one connector out. See Adding and Connecting Activities for more information.
