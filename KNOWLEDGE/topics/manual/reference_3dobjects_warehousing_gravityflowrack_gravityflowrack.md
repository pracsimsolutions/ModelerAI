# Gravity Flow Rack

## Overview and Key Concepts

 

The Gravity Flow Rack is a subclass of the Rack, which is in turn a subclass of the Storage Object. Most logic for the Gravity Flow Rack is implemented as part of the Storage Object class. The main specializations of the Gravity Flow Rack are as follows:

 

1. A gravity flow rack's "place face" is the opposite of its pick face. In other words, task executers will place items on the rack on the opposite side as the side they pick from.
1. A gravity flow rack will animate the flow of items from the place face to the pick face, using the user-defined Flow Speed property.
